

Preventative Maintenance: scheduled routine work to reduce the chance of failure
Predictive Maintenance: using condition data, sensors or trends to service something before it fails
Corrective Maintnenance: fixing an issue that was found before it caused full failure
Reactive Maintenance: repairing only after something breaks
Condition-based Maintenance: maintenance triggered by actual asset condition, not just time or usage
Routine Maintenance: basic recurring upkeep like cleaning, lubrication, tightening, inspections
